# 📊 AtliQ Mart Promotional Campaign Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview

The AtliQ Mart Promotional Campaign Analysis Dashboard is a Power BI project developed to evaluate the effectiveness of promotional campaigns across different cities, stores, products, and categories. The dashboard helps analyze how promotional strategies impact sales performance and product demand.

This project focuses on key promotional performance metrics such as Incremental Revenue (IR), Incremental Revenue Percentage (IR%), Incremental Sold Units (ISU), and ISU Percentage, providing insights into the effectiveness of various promotional strategies and marketing campaigns.

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators

- Revenue Before Promotion – Total revenue generated before promotional campaigns.
- Revenue After Promotion – Total revenue generated after applying promotional campaigns.
- IR (Incremental Revenue) – Additional revenue generated due to promotional activities.
- IR % (Incremental Revenue Percentage) – Percentage increase in revenue driven by promotions.
- ISU (Incremental Sold Units) – Additional units sold due to promotional campaigns.
- ISU % (Incremental Sold Units Percentage) – Percentage increase in units sold during promotions.

These metrics help measure the real impact of promotional campaigns on business performance.

### 📊 Dashboard Pages
1️. Campaign Performance Overview

The overview page provides a high-level view of promotional campaign performance.

Key insights include:

- Comparison of revenue before and after promotions
- City-wise revenue performance during campaigns
- Category-level promotional impact
- Promotion effectiveness across different regions

2️. Store Performance Analysis

This section analyzes campaign performance at the store level.

Key insights include:

- Top performing stores based on incremental revenue
- Bottom performing stores with lower promotion impact
- Store-level incremental sales performance
- Comparison of IR% and ISU% across stores

3️. Promotion Type Analysis

This page focuses on analyzing the performance of different promotional strategies.

Key insights include:

- Revenue impact of different promotion types
- Incremental units sold across promotion types
- Contribution of promotions such as BOGOF, Cashback, and Discount offers
- Campaign-wise promotion performance comparison

4️. Product & Category Analysis

This section analyzes promotional impact across products and product categories.

Key insights include:

- Product-level incremental revenue and unit growth
- Category-wise promotion performance
- Identification of high-performing and underperforming products
- Relationship between promotion type, product category, and sales growth
